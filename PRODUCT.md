# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Small business owners who manage manual orders — balloon arrangements or otherwise. They check orders on their phone throughout the day, often between production work. Quick glance, quick action. The tool replaces a paper notebook or spreadsheet.

## Product Purpose

A no-fuss order rail that tracks what needs making, when it needs shipping, and who's paid. One person runs it. Speed and reliability over features.

## Brand Personality

Clean, focused, reliable. The tool gets out of the way. Warmth comes from the dark palette and craft feel, not from personality in the copy. Professional but not corporate. Friendly but not playful.

## Anti-references

- Anything that looks like a generic SaaS dashboard (stark white sidebar, blue primary, data tables everywhere)
- Over-designed "craft" or "handmade" aesthetics (rough textures, illustrated elements, paper textures)
- Apps that use excessive modals, nested cards, or complex navigation
- Bright saturated colors that signal "fun" — this is a work tool

## Design Principles

- **Start from the work, not the chrome.** Every screen prioritizes the next action. Navigation and branding are minimal.
- **One job at a time.** Each view serves a single purpose. No split attention.
- **Dark warmth, not dark cool.** The palette is warm-earth, not dark-blue-terminal. Feels like a workshop, not a command line.
- **Prefer taps over types.** Production workers have dirty hands. Big targets, minimal text input.
- **Everything visible, nothing lurking.** No hover-revealed actions, no hidden menus. What you can do, you can see.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA contrast for all text
- Large touch targets (44px minimum)
- Reduced motion preference respected
- Works in portrait orientation on phones
